The Impact of a Successful Attack





1) CONFIDENTIALITY. A successful can gain access to all internal information of a Database. Email, phone number, personal information, credentials, etc. Knowing some of this data the attacker could access to other applications on the system. So, confidenticality is leaking internal data.
2) INTEGRITY. Once a secuence of attack has been successfull, the attacker can add accounts, change database structure, delete data, inject malicious scripts etc. Or simply the attacker could delete the entire database.
3) AVAILABILITY/ In this case the attacker can turn the system off line, delete files, destroy all the data. So the risky is high enough that could turn an entire system off.
